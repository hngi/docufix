ABOUT US

Docufix is an app that aids in curbing plagiarism and copy leaks, it can be used by students, lecturers and researchers alike. The aim of docufix is to stop and reduce the act of taking credit for the written work of others. This will be very helpful to writers and columnists, editors, professionals in the printing, writing and educational industry at large.
